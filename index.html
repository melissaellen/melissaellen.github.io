Hi, I'm Melissa — I design with empathy and ship with confidence.

I'm a UX designer with a background in cognitive psychology and a deep love for research. I believe the best design is invisible — it just feels right. I specialize in discovery, systems thinking, and making complicated things feel simple.

When I'm not pushing pixels in Figma, I'm running user interviews, facilitating design sprints, or convincing developers that just a few more pixels of breathing room really does matter.
